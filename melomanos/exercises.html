Piano exercises are usually very tedious to perform. The piano technique causes many students to get discouraged and abandon their piano practice. Exercise doesn't have to be annoying. On the contrary, they should be fun. Learning should be fun so that we can love what we study. If there is something we do not understand, it is not healthy to remain in doubt. In the question and answer section, you can find the clarification of many basic principles in music so that you can continue with your practice. We encourage you to seek the help of the metronome to keep the rhythm and beats, according to the exercise or the song. There are so many options that we will have no excuse not to continue. Discipline beats intelligence. The established and fulfilled schedules turn any student into a master.
