Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to publicize the places of the most visited rapids in the mountains. There are activities for the whole family to check out and decide to have a guided tour on this wonderful adventure. There are great sites to capture the most beautiful photos as if it were a postcard.

Audience

This site is mainly aimed at young adults, but there are exciting activities for the whole family without taking any risks. In addition, there are ideal places to do teenage camps.
